Who invented football?

Football was invented by the English in the 19th century. They set the rules, the dimensions of the field and the goals, and the number of players. The first Football World Cup was organized in 1930 and won by Uruguay.

Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi

The Messi_Ronaldo rivalry, or Ronaldo_Messi rivalry, is a sporting rivalry in football propelled by the media and fans that involves Argentine footballer Lionel Messi and Portuguese footballer Cristiano Ronaldo, mainly for being contemporaries and for their similar records and sporting success.[1] They spent nine seasons in the prime of their careers facing off regularly while playing for rival clubs Barcelona and Real Madrid.
